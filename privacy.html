Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE DATE: 2025-01-01  ·  LAST UPDATED: 2025-06-01

01 — TL;DR

NON-CUSTODIAL. YOUR KEYS, YOUR DATA.

Forge-OS never has access to your private keys or seed phrase. All cryptographic secrets are encrypted on your device and never leave it. We do not run any server that stores your wallet data.

02 — WHO WE ARE

Forge-OS is a non-custodial browser wallet and Web3 interface for the Kaspa blockchain network. The software is provided as a browser extension and as a web application available at forge-os.xyz.

For privacy questions, contact us at: privacy@forge-os.xyz

04 — WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT

  • We do not collect your seed phrase, private keys, or passwords.
  • We do not operate any server that receives or stores your wallet secrets.
  • We do not track your browsing history or activity on other websites.
  • We do not use analytics, telemetry, or crash-reporting SDKs that phone home.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties for advertising.
  • We do not require account registration or an email address.

05 — ENCRYPTION & SECURITY MODEL

Your seed phrase is encrypted using AES-256-GCM with a key derived from your password via PBKDF2-SHA-256 (600,000 iterations) before being written to browser storage. The plaintext mnemonic exists only in memory while the wallet is actively unlocked and is wiped immediately on lock.

Key derivation uses a random 256-bit salt generated fresh for every vault write, so no two encrypted vaults are identical even with the same password.

YOUR RESPONSIBILITY

Because we have no access to your keys, we cannot recover your wallet if you forget your password or lose your seed phrase. Back up your seed phrase in a safe, offline location.

06 — NETWORK REQUESTS

Forge-OS communicates with the following external services to provide wallet functionality. No personally identifiable information is transmitted beyond your public wallet address:

  • api.kaspa.org (and testnet equivalents) — Kaspa public REST API. Used to fetch UTXO sets, account balance, and broadcast signed transactions. Only your public address is sent.
  • Kaspa price data — fetched from public market data APIs. No wallet address is included in these requests.

These third-party APIs have their own privacy policies. Forge-OS does not control how they handle request metadata (e.g. IP address).
